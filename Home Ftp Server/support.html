Home and Simple Series Freeware Products Support

Both Home and Simple Series freeware products has support page at downstairs.dnsalias.net on my home computer running the Home Web Server. Page contains lots of information about these products and also support forum where you may find answers to questions you may have or share idea of what would be nice to have feature on the products in future.

As the support page is being served here with the latest information, I've secondary download page at another place, which is always online and even all the information cannot be found there, the product installer packages are always updated there also.

Updates

Even most of the products do have internet update possibility, it's sometimes very nice to see what has changed. Everything changed is added into the revision history document published below the actual product download link. In case there will be big changes to implement into the product, there is always beta revision available for download for sime time to make sure everything that is changed didn't cause any harm to previous functionality and to confirm the new features functionality before the actual revision is released as final. This is also because the internet update will after this use the final build.
